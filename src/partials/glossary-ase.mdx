An ASE is an entity that provides and maintains payment accounts for its customers, and is a regulated entity in the country or countries it operates. Examples include digital wallet providers, banks, mobile money providers, and crypto wallets. 

An ASE's responsibilities include:

* Onboarding account holders as required under regulations (such as KYC and sanctions screenings)
* Maintaining account ledgers
* Handling account withdrawals and deposits
* Authenticating account holders and obtaining their consent for third-parties to access their accounts
