The Interledger test wallet is an open-source application developed and maintained by the Interledger Foundation. With the test wallet, you can send and receive Web Monetization payments without using real money.

Sign up for a test wallet account, then create a wallet address for a USD payment account.

## Sign up for an Interledger test wallet account

1. Go to <LinkOut href="https://rafiki.money">Rafiki.money</LinkOut>.
2. Click **Create an account** at the bottom-right of the screen.
3. Enter your email address and a password. A verification email will be sent to the address you enter.
4. Open the verification email and click **Confirm my email address**.
5. Sign in to your Interledger test wallet account.
6. Enter anything into the _Complete KYC_ fields. Since this is a test account, you don't need to enter real information.
7. Click **Verify your identity**, then click **Verify Account**.
8. Click **Go to your account overview**.

## Create a USD payment account and WM wallet address

1. Sign in to your <LinkOut href="https://rafiki.money">Interledger test wallet</LinkOut> account.
2. Click **New account** on the _Home_ page.
3. Enter an account name, select **USD** as the asset, then click **Create account**.
   :::note
   Web Monetization can only be enabled on USD accounts.
   :::

   <img
     src='/img/docs/testnet/01.png'
     class='img-outline'
     style='max-width:500px'
   />

4. Select the account, then click **Add money**. Enter any amount and click **Add money** again. You can always add more later.
   <img
     src='/img/docs/testnet/02.png'
     class='img-outline'
     style='max-width:500px'
   />
5. Click the **Web Monetization** link located above and to the right of your balance.
   <img
     src='/img/docs/testnet/03.png'
     class='img-outline'
     style='max-width:500px'
   />
6. Click **Add WM payment pointer**.
   <img
     src='/img/docs/testnet/04.png'
     class='img-outline'
     style='max-width:500px'
   />
7. Enter a [payment pointer](/docs/resources/glossary#payment-pointer) name and a public name of your choosing, then click **Create**.
   :::note[Payment pointer name]
   The name you choose will make up your wallet address. In the image below, the wallet address will be `https://ilp.rafiki.money/melissawm`. You'll need this address to <mark>support web monetized content</mark> and/or to [web monetize your own content](/docs/implement/link).
   :::
   <img
     src='/img/docs/testnet/05.png'
     class='img-outline'
     style='max-width:500px'
   />
8. Verify your wallet address appears under the _Account_ section.
   <img
     src='/img/docs/testnet/06.png'
     class='img-outline'
     style='max-width:500px'
   />

## Create a WM wallet address for an existing USD payment account

1. Sign in to your <LinkOut href="https://rafiki.money">Interledger test wallet</LinkOut> account.
2. Select your USD account on the _Home_ page.
3. Click the **Web Monetization** link located above and to the right of your balance.
